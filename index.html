Caring for Fancy Mice

Mice are easy to care for and make incredibly rewarding companions.

This care guide is an accumulation of facts and tips from various resources including the Animal Humane Society, Petfinder, The Spruce Pets, VetBabble, RSPCA, and personal experience.

Do your research and know what you’re getting yourself into before bringing your furry friend—or friends—home!
